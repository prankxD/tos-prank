Peak Mic Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Last Updated: April 9, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Peak Mic. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord bot, Peak Mic (the "Bot"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not use the Bot.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy. Your continued use of the Bot after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

Information We Collect

To provide its services, Peak Mic needs certain permissions and access to specific information within your Discord server:

  • Server Member Information:
    • Usernames and Discriminators: To identify you within the server and provide personalized interactions.
    • User IDs: To uniquely identify users and manage interactions specific to them.
    • Roles and Permissions: To ensure the bot operates correctly and securely according to the permissions granted within the server.
  • Message Content:
    • Commands: We read the content of messages specifically directed at Peak Mic as commands (e.g., starting with the bot's prefix) to process those commands.
    • Command Deletion: To maintain a clean chat environment, command messages may be deleted immediately after being processed by the bot.
  • Voice Channel Activity:
    • Connections: We collect information about your connection status and presence in voice channels to play music as requested when you use playback commands.
  • Required Permissions: The bot requires standard Discord permissions like Read Messages, Send Messages, Manage Messages (for command deletion), Connect, and Speak to function correctly. Functionality relies on Discord's Server Member and Message Content intents being enabled for the bot.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: To operate Peak Mic and deliver its core functionalities, such as processing music commands (play, skip, etc.), managing user interactions, and playing audio in voice channels.
  • Improving Services: We may analyze aggregated and anonymized usage data (which does not identify individual users) to understand how the bot is used, identify areas for improvement, enhance performance, and develop new features.
  • Security & Stability: To monitor for abuse, ensure compliance with terms, and maintain the operational integrity of the bot.

Disclosure of Your Information

We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may utilize third-party services necessary for the bot's operation (e.g., hosting providers). These providers are only given access to the information required to perform their services and are obligated to maintain confidentiality. We currently use personal VPS as our hosting.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, respond to legal process, protect and defend our rights or property, prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Service, protect the personal safety of users of the Service or the public, or protect against legal liability.

We are using our own VPS for hosting lavalink and bot to ensure your data is fully safe.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, despite our safeguards and efforts to secure your information, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. We cannot promise or guarantee that hackers, cybercriminals, or other unauthorized third parties will not be able to defeat our security and improperly collect, access, steal, or modify your information.

Data Retention

We retain information collected only for as long as necessary to provide Peak Mic's services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Specifically, command messages directed at the bot are typically deleted immediately after processing to keep chat channels clean. Other data like server configurations or user IDs associated with settings might be retained as long as the bot is active in the server or until requested for deletion according to your rights.

Your Rights / Managing Your Data

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access information we might hold related to your User ID and request correction of any inaccuracies. Please contact us using the details below.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data associated with the bot (e.g., specific settings tied to your User ID). We will comply with such requests unless we have a legitimate basis to retain the data (like legal requirements). Removing the bot from your server will also typically lead to the deletion of server-specific configurations.
  • Managing Bot Presence: You can remove Peak Mic from your server at any time through Discord's interface.

Children's Privacy

Peak Mic is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the relevant age without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.
